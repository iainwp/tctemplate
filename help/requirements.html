TCTemplate Help

Requirements

  • TCTemplate works on any device with a web browser, including most phones and desktop computers, including Windows, MacOS, Linux and Android. However, it sometimes fails in Microsoft Edge, but appears to be reliable in Google Chrome and Safari.
  • To prepare the maps in Purple Pen, Microsoft Windows XP or newer is required, although it should work in a virtual Windows machine on Mac or Linux (it does not work under Wine on Mac).
  • You will need a printer capable of a 10 mm margin on all 4 sides for A4 paper.
